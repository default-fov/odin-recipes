How I really feel about beef tacos

Oh, beef tacos. Where do I even begin with this transcendent combination of flavor, texture, and sheer culinary magic? Imagine, if you will, a warm, soft corn tortilla, cradling the most glorious, perfectly cooked 80/20 ground beef. It’s a balance of fat and lean that melts and sizzles in the pan, its juices mingling with the taco seasoning—oh, that seasoning! The moment it touches the beef, it dissolves into the sizzling meat, creating a rich, savory sauce that clings to every inch of the beef. It's like the spices have kissed each piece individually, the earthy cumin, the smoky paprika, and the touch of garlic and chili powder all swirling together into a savory symphony. As the beef cooks, it browns just enough, leaving the edges crispy and slightly caramelized, while the middle remains juicy and succulent.

And then—oh, and then—the toppings. The reduced-fat Mexican-style cheese, oh, that cheese, it’s like the perfect blanket, slowly melting over the hot beef, its creamy texture combining with the beef’s richness in a way that’s nothing short of pure euphoria. Nestled delicately atop that glorious cheese is the lettuce, crisp and vibrant, its cool crunch providing the most satisfying contrast to the warm, savory beef. The lettuce, so simple yet vital, gives each bite a refreshing pop, a crispness that’s a counterpoint to the richness of the beef and the smooth, melty cheese. And then, just as if fate itself were guiding this taco to perfection, the salsa—oh, the salsa! A vibrant, lively mix of tomatoes, zesty jalapeños, smoky chipotles, and the sharp bite of red onion—each ingredient brings a new layer of flavor. The tomato’s juiciness is like the very essence of summer, the jalapeños kick things up a notch with a delicate heat, while the chipotles lend a smoky depth that’s impossible to resist.

Biting into this taco? It’s a moment of blissful, unadulterated joy. The beef, the cheese, the lettuce, the salsa—all of it melding together in your mouth, each flavor and texture complementing the next in an orchestral dance of culinary perfection. It’s like every single element was made to be with the others, an unforgettable experience that you’ll yearn for again as soon as the last bite is gone. There’s something truly sacred about a taco like this—it’s more than food, it’s an experience, a little piece of heaven wrapped up in a tortilla. If I could describe the taste of happiness, it would be this taco.

Let's make a few taco's, shall we?

  • Corn Tortilla: Soft and slightly sweet, the perfect vessel for all the savory goodness that follows, with just the right amount of chew.
  • 80/20 Beef: Rich, juicy, and tender, with the ideal fat-to-lean ratio, giving each bite an indulgent, meaty satisfaction.
  • Reduced Fat Mexican-Style Cheese: Creamy, melty, and perfectly sharp, it adds a luscious, gooey layer of flavor that complements the beef perfectly.
  • Salsa (Tomato, Jalapeño, Chipotles, Red Onion): A vibrant mix of tangy tomatoes, spicy jalapeños, smoky chipotles, and sharp red onion, creating a bold, zesty kick.
  • Lettuce: Crisp, fresh, and cool, offering a crunchy contrast to the rich beef and cheese.

Here's how to use these ingredients to make these succulent, succulent, taco's.

  1. Brown your beef a bit until the juices collect, once they collect, drain the beef from the pan, return, and continue cooking and mixing your beef before adding your taco seasoning.
  2. Take your cooked beef and put it into the taco shell.
  3. Apply lettuce first. Contrary to what people may think, the lettuce doesn't 'wilt' to the heat of the beef, and it keeps the cheese from melting. I like my cheese either melted or not.
  4. Repeat until satisfied with amount of taco's on a single plate.
  5. Fill a 6oz bowl with a gracious amount of freshly minced, blended, and juiced pico-style salsa and top with salt to taste.
  6. Enjoy!
